Support Resources

Nova Scotia Provincial Mental Health and Addictions Crisis Line:

1-888-429-8167

Kids Help Phone:

1-800-668-6868

Text: 686868

Professional counselling, information, and referrals support to young people. Available 24/7.

Drug Rehab Services:

1-877-254-3348

Free, confidential professional help and resource for drug and alcohol addiction in Canada. Referrals for clients seeking support with substances.

CAPSA Peer Support:

https://capsa.ca/peer-support-meetings/

Free peer-facilitated group meetings (includes virtual meetings). Evidence-based practices and tools designed to help those who are questioning their relationship with substances.

Alcoholics Anonymous:

https://www.aa.org/find-aa

Free meetings and support for people who come together to solve their drinking problem.

Narcotics Anonymous:

https://na.org/meetingsearch/

1-855-562-2262

Free meetings (includes virtual) and support for anyone who wants to stop using drugs. This is a program of complete abstinence from all drugs.

SMART Recovery:

https://meetings.smartrecovery.org/meetings/

Free support meetings (in-person and virtual) open to anyone seeking science-based, self-empowered addiction recovery.

Holding Hope:

https://www.holdinghopecanada.org/find-a-group

Free peer-led support groups for families with loved ones living with addiction.

Families for Addiction Recovery:

1-855-377-6677

https://www.farcanada.org/family-support/we-can-help/parent-support-group/

https://www.farcanada.org/family-support/we-can-help/parent-parent-support/

Free peer support services for families, parents, caregivers of children struggling with addiction.

Provincial and Territorial Quit Smoking Services:

1-866-366-3667

Access free, convenient support within each province and territory. Telephone, online, or text services in English and French based on location. Trained specialists can provide counselling, help develop a quit smoking plan, answer questions, and provide referrals to programs and services in your community.
